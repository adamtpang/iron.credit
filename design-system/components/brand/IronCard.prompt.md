The Iron card — a matte gunmetal Visa with engine-turned guilloché engraving and the I-beam mark. Built in CSS/SVG, scales from `width`.

```jsx
<IronCard width={420} holder="S. ARRYN" last4="4271" exp="09 / 29" />
<IronCard width={300} finish="edge" virtual />   {/* orange machined edge, virtual tag */}
```

Finishes: `guilloche` (engraved, flagship default), `brushed` (vertical grain), `edge` (single machined orange stripe). Keep it on dark surfaces with room to breathe.
