# Iron Company OS

The operating system for running Iron like a game with a scoreboard. Strategy and the "why" live in `MASTERPLAN.md`. This file is the cockpit: mission, the one number, the dashboard, the gated roadmap, the team, and the cadence. Modeled on cofounder.co (gated roadmap + dual-mode dashboard), Paperclip (Mission to Goal to Project to Ticket, each with one owner), and the EOS weekly review.

---

## Layer 1: Mission, North Star, Goals

**Mission (never changes):** Become the credit layer of the bitcoin economy. Let anyone spend their bitcoin's value without selling it, non-custodial and radically transparent.

**The problem (one breath):** Bitcoiners are asset-rich and cash-poor by choice (selling means tax and lost upside), and billions more are rejected by banks entirely. Bitcoin is the universal collateral that solves both. Every prior attempt was custodial, opaque, or a yield scam, and they died (see `GRAVEYARD.md`).

**North Star Metric (this stage):** qualified waitlist signups (people who genuinely want the card). It flips to **active borrowers with a live balance** once the product is live.

**Goals this quarter (3, max 5):**
1. A working card-at-POS prototype, filmed (the milestone that unlocks capital).
2. A cofounder onboard, or a strong first hire in the seat.
3. Singapore Pte Ltd incorporated and the pre-seed conversation open.

## Layer 5: The game (gamify only three things)

Evidence says minimal gamification beats a cluttered one. Gamify only **input metrics you actually want more of.** No leaderboards (meaningless solo), no trivial badges.
1. **Progress bar** on the current stage (the `0/7` completion fraction above).
2. **A meaningful streak:** consecutive days you did at least one real input action (a customer call, a ship, or a build-in-public post). It counts an input you control, never a vanity number.
3. **The weekly scorecard:** inputs vs target, reviewed every week (below).

---

## Layer 6: Operating cadence

- **Daily heartbeat (10 min, ties to your /start routine):** What one outcome makes today a win? Update yesterday's input numbers, pick today's 1 to 3 tickets, note blockers.
- **Weekly review (60 to 90 min, EOS Level 10 agenda):** wins → scorecard vs target → goal progress → did I do last week's to-dos → identify, discuss, solve the top 1 to 3 issues → set next week's tickets → write one paragraph on what moved and why.
- **Quarterly:** reset the 3 to 5 goals, re-pick the North Star if the stage changed.
